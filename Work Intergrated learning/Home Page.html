Skills Training for Domestic Workers and Gardeners

Empowering the Nation was established in 2018 and offers courses in Johannesburg. Hundreds of domestic workers and gardeners have been trained on both the six-month long Learnerships and six-week Short Skills Training Programmes to empower themselves and to provide more marketable skills.
